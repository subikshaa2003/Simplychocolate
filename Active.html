Our strongest family members

Simply® Rich Arnold, Simply® Sixpack Sally, and Simply® Speedy Tom are gluten-free and made with the
finest natural ingredients, 25% protein, and we've steered clear of artificial sweeteners, flavors, and
colorings. We simply believe that real ingredients and natural flavors taste better. Taste the difference and
find your new favorite bar.













Caramel, peanuts and premium dark chocolate

Simply® Rich Arnold

Behind a thick layer of premium dark chocolate, you'll find a core of peanuts, caramel, and sweet dates. Arnold is our bestseller - and we understand why. Enjoy 25% protein without compromising on the high quality and great taste.










Caramel, yuzu, sesame and premium milk chocolate

Simply® Sixpack Sally

Surrounded by premium milk chocolate, you will find a core
consisting of caramel crunch, yuzu, and sesame. The combination
of the sweet milk chocolate and the aromatic yuzu delivers a flavor
experience beyond the ordinary.










Acai, cocoa, passionfruit and premium dark chocolate

Simply® Speedy Tom

Tom is a sure winner for most. The flavor of cocoa, acai, and passion
fruit, coated with a thick layer of premium dark chocolate, will
undoubtedly elevate your refined taste for natural protein bars.
